## Standards

Standards are drafted/published by standards bodies.

Types of standards:
- Knowledge and Skills
- Conduct
- Accounting
- Quality
- Measurement precision

### Impact accounting standards

The Independent Impact Platform does not prescribe any specific impact accounting standard; it does, however, use the XXX Standard as its default impact accounting standard.

Standards specify principles. 
Standards should provide measures (questions) to activity owners to guide them towards adherence to their principles.
Standards should provide reviewers with measures (questions) to guide them in their evaluation of an activity's adherence to the standard's principles.
The platform's review process is designed to award high scores to activities that adhere to the standard's principles.
