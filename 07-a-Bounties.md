## Bounties

Bounties are a mechanism for incentivizing specific activities or improvements on the Independent Impact platform. They allow agents to offer rewards for the completion of particular tasks, validation of claims, or provision of expertise.

### Bounty Creator CR Gain Formula

The creator of a bounty will earn CR (Credibility Rating) proportionate to the duration of the bounty and the size of the reward. The CR gain for bounty creators is calculated using the following formula:

**CR_gain = (Reward_Size × Duration_Factor × Base_Multiplier) / 1000**

Where:
- **Reward_Size**: The total value of the bounty reward in platform tokens
- **Duration_Factor**: A multiplier based on bounty duration:
  - Short-term (≤ 7 days): 1.0
  - Medium-term (8-30 days): 1.2
  - Long-term (31-90 days): 1.5
  - Extended (> 90 days): 2.0
- **Base_Multiplier**: A constant value of 10 to ensure meaningful CR gains

The division by 1000 normalizes the result to prevent excessive CR inflation while maintaining proportionality to the bounty's economic value and time commitment.

### Additional Bounty Mechanics

Bounty creators demonstrate their commitment to platform improvement by allocating resources and time to incentivize valuable contributions. The CR gain reflects this positive contribution to the ecosystem while encouraging the creation of meaningful, well-scoped bounties that benefit the broader community.

The formula ensures that larger, longer-duration bounties provide proportionally higher CR rewards, recognizing the greater investment and risk undertaken by their creators.
