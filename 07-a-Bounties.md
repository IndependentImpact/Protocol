## Bounties

### General

A bounty on the Independent Impact platform functions similarly to its real-world counterpart: it involves a sum of money locked in a smart contract, accompanied by a task description. Agents who complete the task can claim the funds. Any agent with sufficient funds can create a bounty for various tasks, including data gathering, information review, or indicator creation. Bounties are generally open, allowing any agent who meets the specified requirements to participate.

Unlike traditional bounties, those on the Independent Impact platform limit the number of agents who can pursue a bounty simultaneously. Small tasks typically allow only one agent at a time, while larger tasks may permit multiple agents to collaborate rather than compete. This limitation serves two key purposes:

(b) Prevents unnecessary resource wastage. Please elaborate.

### Mission Team

A mission team consists of agents who collaborate to complete a bounty. The team is formed during the team assembly phase.

### Phases
